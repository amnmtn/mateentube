About YouTube

YouTube, a social media platform and website for sharing videos, was registered on February 14, 2005, by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim, three former employees of the American e-commerce company PayPal. They had the idea that ordinary people would enjoy sharing their “home videos.” The company is headquartered in San Bruno, California.

YouTube is a video-sharing platform where users can watch, like, share, comment, and upload their own videos.

Founded in February 2005 by former PayPal employees, YouTube was acquired by Google in November 2006 for $1.65 billion.

Today, YouTube is one of the most popular websites on the internet, with billions of users and a wide variety of content ranging from educational videos to entertainment.
